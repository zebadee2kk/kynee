## Decision-Making Process

### Day-to-Day Decisions

- **Bug fixes**: Merged by any maintainer after review
- **Minor features**: Merged by maintainer with relevant expertise
- **Documentation**: Merged by any maintainer

### Significant Decisions

Require consensus of at least 2 core team members:

- Major architectural changes
- Breaking API changes
- Security policy modifications
- Changes to governance structure
- Addition/removal of core team members

### Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)

Significant technical decisions are documented as ADRs in `docs/architecture/`.

- Proposed by any contributor
- Reviewed by Architecture Lead
- Accepted by Lead Maintainer
- Superseded ADRs are marked as such (never deleted)

## Contribution Process

1. **Propose**: Open an issue or discussion
2. **Discuss**: Community feedback
3. **Implement**: Create a PR following CONTRIBUTING.md
4. **Review**: Maintainer review (code, tests, docs)
5. **Merge**: Squash/rebase merge by maintainer

---

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors may be invited to join the core team based on:

- **Sustained contributions**: 10+ merged PRs over 3+ months
- **Quality**: High-quality code, tests, documentation
- **Community**: Helpful in reviews, discussions, and issues
- **Ethics**: Strong adherence to ETHICAL_USE_POLICY.md

Invitation process:

1. Nominated by existing maintainer
2. Consensus among core team
3. Invitation sent via private message
4. Public announcement in GitHub Discussions

---

## Code of Conduct Enforcement

Violations of CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md are handled by:

1. **Warning**: Private message from maintainer
2. **Temporary ban**: 7-30 days (no PRs, comments, or issues)
3. **Permanent ban**: Severe or repeated violations

Appeals:

- Email governance@[project-domain] (to be established)
- Reviewed by core team (excluding involved parties)
- Final decision by Lead Maintainer

---

## Security Policy Enforcement

Maintainers may:

- Close PRs that violate ETHICAL_USE_POLICY.md
- Ban users who advocate for unauthorized use
- Report illegal activity to authorities

See SECURITY.md for vulnerability disclosure process.

---

## Conflict Resolution

1. **Discussion**: Attempt to resolve in PR/issue comments
2. **Mediation**: Uninvolved maintainer mediates
3. **Vote**: Core team votes (simple majority)
4. **Final say**: Lead Maintainer breaks ties

---

## Changes to Governance

This document may be amended by:
